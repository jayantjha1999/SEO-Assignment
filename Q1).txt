Q1)What is SEO and explain the importance of SEO?

Ans-SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing a website or online content to improve its visibility and ranking on search engine results pages (SERPs). The goal of SEO is to increase organic (non-paid) traffic to a website by enhancing its relevance and authority in the eyes of search engines.
Importance of SEO:

Increased Visibility and Traffic:
SEO improves a website's ranking on search engines, making it more likely to be seen by users when they search for relevant topics. Higher visibility leads to increased organic traffic.

Credibility and Trust:
Websites that appear at the top of search results are often perceived as more credible and trustworthy by users. SEO helps build and maintain this credibility.

Better User Experience:
SEO involves optimizing various aspects of a website, leading to a better user experience. This includes faster loading times, mobile optimization, and high-quality content.

Cost-Effectiveness:
Organic search traffic generated through SEO is essentially free, making it a cost-effective way to attract visitors compared to paid advertising.
