Q5)Define Favicon and give an illustrative example.
Ans-#Favicon
A favicon (short for favorite icon) is a small, typically square image or icon associated with a website. Favicons are displayed in the browser's address bar, next to the site's name in bookmarks or favorites, and in browser tabs. 
Ex-
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html lang="en">
<head>
<meta charset="UTF-8">
<meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
<title>BikeService - Your Online Bike service</title>
<!-- Favicon -->
<link rel="icon" href="favicon.ico" type="image/x-icon">
</head>
<body>
</body>
